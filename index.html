Welcome!

Welcome to my personal website! I recently finished my PhD in Cognition & Perception at the Psychology Department of New York University, working with Dr. Jonathan Winawer and collaborating with Dr. Marisa Carrasco. I'm excited to continue my research as a postdoc in the Stanford Vision and Perception Neuroscience Lab, working with Dr. Kalanit Grill-Spector.

I'm interested in visual perception and its underlying neural mechanisms. For example, how is visual information processed at the different stages along visual pathway? And how do these processing steps shape our visual perception? I use psychophysics, eye tracking, MRI, EEG/MEG techniques in combination with computational modeling to answer my research questions.
