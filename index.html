VSS Poster and cone absorption video's!

I'm excited to present my work at this year's Annual Vision Science Society Meeting in St. Pete Beach, FL. This project uses a computational observer model of the front-end of the visual system to test how much pre-neural factors contribute to differences in visual performance on various psychophysical taks.
